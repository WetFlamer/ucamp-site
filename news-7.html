The rise of a new era, outdoor social activities

People in the outdoor field make fun of 2020 as the "first year of camping". "In fact, as early as 2020, we have seen more and more tents in our circle of friends. After the epidemic, a large number of people went out of the city and entered the countryside to start a diversified outdoor life. They felt the social space of" temperature "and" smoke and fire "in nature, and the pure friendship between people and their infinite love for nature deeply touched them.

Outdoor life is not only a way of life, but also a life attitude.

Ucamp is positioned as the "new lifestyle" in the Z era, which is simply to connect diverse lifestyles, products and different regions, cultures and people through exquisite camping.

In festivals and holidays, camping has become the travel choice of many people, and exquisite camping has swept in like a hurricane. Due to the epidemic prevention and control, peripheral tours and short trips are the first choice for people to travel during the holiday, while camping in peripheral tours has become a new way for more and more people to play. Compared with traditional camping, exquisite camping has more quality and trend. In contrast with the tent camping and picnic with simple and simple conditions in the past, exquisite camping has a lot of tools and accessories to improve the experience. It not only meets a high-quality dialogue with nature, but also pays more attention to the sense of life ceremony and improves the sense of physical and mental experience.


Stars at night, birdsong on the lawn, tent campfire, retro lanterns, round table folding chairs, barbecue rack, coffee machine Accommodation and catering are displayed in special forms, escaping from the hustle and bustle of the city, and restoring the desired lifestyle at the edge of the city that is close to nature. What is attractive about the exquisite camping is its unique ability to create life interest. The scenery is in front of us, and life is at hand.
