"A New Journey to Zhongshan" | Nanlang · Chongkou · UCAMP Camp

Zhongshan Xinjin - Exquisite Campsite

According to the report of the column "Tasting the New Journey of Zhongshan" jointly produced by Zhongshan Municipal Bureau of Culture, Radio, Television and Tourism and Zhongshan Radio and Television Station,

In 2022, Nanlang Street won the provincial leisure agriculture and rural tourism demonstration town in 2021.

Nanlang Rural Tourism Resort has successfully applied to become the third batch of "Guangdong Provincial Rural Tourism Boutique Routes".

In 1:32 - 2:36 of the video of "New Journey to Zhongshan", the Ucamp Star Camp was selected, edited and broadcast by the program team.


Under the tide of rural revitalization, Chongkou Village has created many rural tourism routes according to local conditions. Located in the Ucamp Starship Camp in the mountains, the original site of the Starship Camp is a abandoned quarry, with exposed land, serious ecological loss and disordered environment. According to the "ecological restoration plan" formulated by the Group, we will firmly inject new life into the "abandoned quarry". The planning design and operation team will design the romantic and fashionable space capsule camp with errors and falls according to the original geographical characteristics, and the new industrial style and natural environment will be organically integrated without any violation, so that the landscape that looks like a landscape device will finally return to the landscape, including the boundless landscape swimming pool, camping and picnic area Children's recreation area and other complex spaces, with more than 300 kinds of plant garden scenery carefully created by landscape designers, make you feel like you are in a paradise.


Under the guidance of the rural revitalization policy and the support of the government departments, the former abandoned quarries will be revitalized and revitalized by the Ucamp Star Camp, and will be built into a pioneering brand base. We boldly seek the balance between nature and technology, history and era, and people and environment, and actively explore the camping lifestyle suitable for Chinese young people.

The unique natural environment has enabled Chongkou Village to have a new exploration and development in creating a new business form of cultural tourism consumption. How can you miss the beautiful scenery and perfect facilities here if you like to "sigh the world"!
