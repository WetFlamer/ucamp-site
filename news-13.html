Lifestyle in the Z epoch

After experiencing the baptism of the epidemic, release the pressure in life and work, stay away from the cold city of steel and cement, and take a delicate "camping" trip.

Experience the relaxation and comfort brought by the diversified lifestyle in the Z era, which makes people feel beautiful and cherish. In this coming autumn season, Starship Camp has also arranged a winter trip for everyone.

All you need to do is bring the size of a family, come to the quiet lakeside mountains, come to the comfortable nature, let the space capsule of the Starship Camp take you to experience the "NEW Lifestyle" holiday life!



Sitting on the ground on the spacious lawn, setting up a camping tent with the family, laying a flat net red wind mat, facing the breeze of the lake and tasting the delicious food made by my mother, the relaxed and comfortable mood becomes up!

In the cool lakeside, face the light of the lake with your friends, bring a cup of mellow coffee/tea, talk freely and enjoy slow life.

You can also use the camera to record the details of camping, show up in the most relaxed state, integrate with nature, and leave a beautiful camping moment.

When night falls, the little lights on the grass, the dark green green hills surrounding the valley, and three or five friends play guitar and sing songs together, adding some romantic feelings to the beautiful camping life.

You can also look up at the starry sky on the grass under the night, lie in the space capsule and listen to the insects in the valley, and experience the openness and nature of "people in the scenery, scenery in people".




Nature, life, vacation, contract your whole year's happy camping life!

The open grassland and the cool lakeside of Shikeng Lake provide an ideal place for exquisite camping.

What the Starship Camp can give you is not only the natural space, but also the light and simple travel space capsule. The tourist service center can provide daily services.

You just need to take the people who love you and the people you love, and you can embark on a happy and exquisite camping trip!

The starship camp surrounded by natural environment invites every outdoor traveler to experience the joy and comfort of camping in wild luxury!
