Joining and signing of Yingde Xiaojiangshan Star Camp

Ucamp carries out the "Land Restoration Plan" according to local conditions, fully utilizes the advantages of idle ecological resources. Under the tide of rural revitalization, Starship Camp helps rural revitalization, and only ecological revitalization can industrial revitalization. In combination with building an exquisite capsule camping camp, it can empower local industries and talents, revitalize villages and towns, implement common prosperity and rebirth, and achieve "social, economic and ecological benefits". It has injected new impetus into regional economic development. "As a newly emerging travel method, exquisite camping tour is of great significance to the development of rural tourism and the revitalization of rural areas.".


With the rise of Ucamp's innovative brand, many cultural and tourism investors expressed great appreciation for the innovative and developable model and strong development potential of the Ucamp project. On this auspicious occasion, at the trial launch of the Starship Camp, the Yingde Xiaojiangshan Starship Camp and Ucamp Starship Camp officially signed a partnership to jointly promote new rural tourism and sustainable economic development.
