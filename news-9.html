Space capsule+micro vacation - opening a new way of life

Starting from downtown Zhongshan and Zhuhai, you can drive less than 40 minutes to enter the Ucamp Star Camp, which is isolated from the hustle and bustle of the city. Winter is coming. Although there is less bustle in the past, the quiet, which is somewhat isolated from the hustle and bustle, has attracted many outdoor lovers who like camping to visit. You can live poetically, feel the sun, the mountain wind, and look up at the stars, allowing your body and mind to have a full rest in the slow pace of life.

Exquisite and unique camping is not exclusive to summer, and camping in winter can also be fun.

On a sunny afternoon, set up a tent on the platform and lawn of the capsule to adjust the mood to the state of vacation, and the exquisite camping will start!

Open the oven and warm the barbecue. It is both exercise and game. Immersion in it, I found that the world of putting down the mobile phone is so interesting.


Boil a pot of hot red wine. When the red wine, together with cloves, cinnamon, anise and oranges, slightly boils in the pot, people's sense of smell, taste and vision will harvest a warm and stable.

Or grind a cup of hot coffee now, take the cup and sit next to the campfire. The crackling sound of the firewood burning is particularly pleasant in the quiet pit lake.

At night, a bonfire rises, a wisp of smoke, a bright moon, and a group of like-minded friends enjoy the unique romance and tranquility of winter camping nights.
